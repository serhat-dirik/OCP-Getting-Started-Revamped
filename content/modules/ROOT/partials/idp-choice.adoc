[NOTE]
====
*Signing in to a platform tool? Choose `workshop-users`.*

This cluster has *two* identity providers wired at once, so OpenShift's sign-in page offers a choice:

* *`workshop-users`* — the one you want. Sign in with your workshop username (`{user}`) and password.
* *`rhbk`* — the Red Hat build of Keycloak. It secures the *sample application* later in the workshop and knows nothing about your workshop account. Choosing it leads to a GitHub-or-guest page that cannot log you in.

If you land on a page offering only *GitHub* or *guest*, go back and pick *`workshop-users`*.
